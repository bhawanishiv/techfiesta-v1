ABOUT GECR

GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE RAMGARH,Run/Managed by TECHNO INDIA established with the prior approval of All India Council For Technical Education(AICTE)and affiliation of Vinoba Bhava University(VBU),is as an institution under Public Private Parternship between the Government of Jharkhand and Techno India Group.

It is situated in the outskirts of Ramgarh district town on NH-23 at Murubanda,near Chittorpur Rajrappa Project covering and area of 24 acres.

The nearest railways station that connects the campus is"MAEL" station and other stations through which the college can be reached are Ramgarh Cantt,Muri Junctionand Ranchi Junction.

It is also well faciliated by bus services from near by cities like Ramgarh,Ranchi,Bokaro,Dhanbad,Kolkatta and Jamshedpur etc.Campus is also nearest to Ranchi Airpot which has good connectivity with daily flights operating for all major cities of India.
